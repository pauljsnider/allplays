Overview

Choose the dashboard that matches what you need to do right now.

  • Use Parent Dashboard for family tasks.
  • Use My Teams Dashboard for team operations.
  • If you have more than one role, switch dashboards by task.

In This Article

  • How to choose the right dashboard quickly.
  • What each dashboard is best for.
  • What to do if teams, players, or actions are missing.

Who Is This For

  • Parents managing linked players and family activity.
  • Coaches managing team operations.
  • Admins supporting or managing team operations.

Prerequisites

  • You are signed in to ALL PLAYS.
  • Your account has the correct role and team/player links.
  • You have at least one linked team or player.

Choose Your Path

  • Choose Parent Dashboard when you need family tools.
  • Choose My Teams Dashboard when you need team management tools.
  • Choose Calendar when you need one combined schedule view across accessible teams.
  • If you are both parent and coach/admin, pick the dashboard based on the task, not the account.

Step-by-Step Workflow

  1. Sign in to ALL PLAYS and confirm you are in the correct account.
  2. Pick your goal: family/player task means Parent Dashboard; team operations task means My Teams Dashboard.
  3. Open the dashboard that matches your goal.
  4. Start work from that page: on Parent Dashboard use My Players, My Teams, or Redeem Code; on My Teams Dashboard open a team card and use View, Chat, Edit, Roster, or Schedule.
  5. Open team details when you need to work in one specific team context.
  6. Open Calendar for cross-team planning, then filter by team, event type, and date range; export .ics when needed.
  7. Check your access level before edits; if actions are read-only, you likely have parent-level access for that team.

Common Questions

Which dashboard should I bookmark?

  • Parents: Parent Dashboard.
  • Coaches/Admins: My Teams Dashboard.

I have parent and coach/admin access. Which one is correct?

Both are correct. Use the dashboard that matches the task.

Can parents open team pages?

Yes. Parents can open team details, but team-management actions may be limited.

Where do I see events across teams?

Use Calendar for combined events and filters.

Recovery & Troubleshooting

No players linked on Parent Dashboard

  • Ask a coach for an invite code.
  • Use Redeem Code.
  • Refresh after successful redemption.

No teams on My Teams Dashboard

  • If you are a coach/admin, use Create New Team.
  • If teams should already exist, verify you are signed in with the correct account.

Team is read-only

  • You likely have parent-linked access for that team.
  • Ask a team owner/admin for coach or admin access if you need edit tools.

Calendar looks empty

  • Adjust or clear filters for team, event type, and date range.
  • Switch between Detailed, Compact, and Calendar views to confirm visibility.

RSVP actions are unavailable

Some calendar-only events must be tracked in the team schedule before availability controls appear.

Need More Help

If the dashboard you see does not match your expected role, contact support and include:

  • Account email
  • Role (Parent, Coach, Admin)
  • Team name(s)
  • The page you opened when the issue happened
  • A screenshot showing which actions are visible and unavailable
