ALL PLAYS Support

For help with access, invitations, schedules, messaging, athlete information, safety, or privacy, email support@allplays.ai.

Before contacting support

  • Include the account email, team name, device type, and the screen where the problem occurred.
  • Describe what you expected and what happened.
  • Attach a screenshot if it does not expose private athlete or family information.
  • Never send a password, login code, payment number, medical details, or government identifier.

Safety and content reports

Use the available report or moderation controls first. For urgent safety concerns, contact the appropriate local emergency or safeguarding authority; ALL PLAYS is not an emergency service.

Account deletion

Open Profile → Security → Delete account in the app, or follow the instructions on the public deletion page.
