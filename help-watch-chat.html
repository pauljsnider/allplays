Watch and Chat

Who owns live viewing and communication actions in production pages.

Watching Live and Replay

  • Parent/Coach/Admin/Member: Open live/replay views via team.html and live-game.html.
  • Coach/Admin: Configure stream source and game live status.
  • Parent/Member: Consume view-only watching experience.

Team Chat

  • Parent/Coach/Admin: Team chat participation in team-chat.html.
  • Coach/Admin: Send operational game-day and scheduling updates.
  • Parent: Parent communication and acknowledgments.

Tagging and Mentions

  • Parent/Coach/Admin: Mention/tag in chat when recipient attention is required.
  • Coach/Admin: Use targeted mentions for assignments and game-day actions.
  • Parent: Use mentions for availability and coordination updates.
